Buko Salad

Home

Description

Buko Salad is a refreshing Filipino dessert made with young coconut meat, milk, and sweetened condensed milk. It's a simple yet delicious treat that's perfect for hot weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup young coconut meat, shredded
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine milk and sweetened condensed milk.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  3. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with a little cold water until smooth.
  4. Gradually add the cornstarch mixture to the boiling milk, stirring continuously.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  7. Let cool to room temperature.
  8. Gently fold in the shredded coconut meat.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Buko Salad!
