Description

Sopas is a comforting Filipino soup made with macaroni, chicken, and vegetables. It's a popular dish, especially during cold weather or when someone is feeling under the weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the carrots, celery, and cabbage. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the shredded chicken and macaroni. Cook until the macaroni is al dente.
  5. Stir in the evaporated milk and condensed milk.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Simmer for a few more minutes until everything is heated through.

Enjoy your delicious Sopas!
